Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Hydration Daily ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our water tracking application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Hydration Logs: Water intake amounts, timestamps, and drink types you record
  • Personal Goals: Your daily hydration goals and preferences
  • Mood & Wellness Data: Optional mood, energy, and focus check-ins
  • Settings: Your app preferences including notification schedules and unit preferences

Data Collected Automatically

  • Location Data (Optional): If you enable weather-based recommendations, we access your approximate location to provide weather data. This is processed in real-time and not stored on our servers.
  • Device Information: Basic device type for app compatibility purposes
Important: All your hydration data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal health data to external servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To track and display your daily water intake
  • To send hydration reminder notifications (if enabled)
  • To calculate personalized hydration recommendations
  • To display your progress, streaks, and achievements
  • To provide weather-adjusted hydration suggestions (if location is enabled)

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using secure SQLite database storage. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

  • All data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up
  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties
  • Location data is used only for real-time weather queries and is not stored

Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Open-Meteo API: For weather data (no personal data shared, only coordinates)
  • RevenueCat: For processing in-app purchases (handles payment securely)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: For app distribution and purchase processing

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by the app (available in app settings)
  • Delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the reset option
  • Disable location access through your device settings
  • Disable notifications at any time
  • Request information about how your data is used

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
