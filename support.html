Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my daily water goal?

Go to Settings > Daily Goal and adjust the slider or enter your preferred amount. The app will automatically update your progress tracking.

How do notifications work?

You can customize reminder frequency and times in Settings > Reminders. The app will send friendly notifications throughout your chosen hours to remind you to drink water.

Can I track drinks other than water?

Yes! Tap the "Change Drink" button on the home screen to select from various beverages like coffee, tea, juice, and more. Each drink type is tracked separately in your history.

How do I cancel my premium subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store. On iOS, go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. On Android, go to Google Play Store > Subscriptions.

Is my data backed up?

Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. We recommend keeping your device backed up through iCloud or Google backup services.

How does weather-based adjustment work?

If you enable location access, the app can suggest increased hydration on hot or humid days. This is optional and your location is only used for weather queries - it's never stored.

What is Habit Stacking?

Habit Stacking links drinking water to activities you already do (like brushing teeth or having meals). When you complete these routines, tap to log water - building hydration into your existing habits!

How do I reset my data?

Go to Settings and scroll down to find the reset option. Please note this action cannot be undone.

Troubleshooting

Notifications not working?

  • Check that notifications are enabled in your device settings
  • Make sure "Do Not Disturb" mode is off during reminder hours
  • Try toggling reminders off and on in app settings

App running slowly?

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Make sure you're running the latest version
  • Restart your device if issues persist

Purchase not recognized?

  • Try the "Restore Purchases" button on the upgrade screen
  • Make sure you're signed into the same App Store/Play Store account
  • Contact us if the issue persists
